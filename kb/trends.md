## Random Graphs Reach the Datacenter Floor
AWS deployed flat random-expander-graph networks in production, solving key challenges with Spraypoint routing, ShuffleBox cabling, and port-by-port operations. This shift promises 69% fewer routers, 33% higher throughput, and lower operating costs compared to hierarchical fat-trees.
