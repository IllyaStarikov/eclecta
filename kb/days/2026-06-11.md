### [Signed Compression Progress on a Sealed Audit is Goodhart-Resistant](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.11417) — 6/10

Notes: Core result: reward r_t = E(θ_{t-1}) − E(θ_t) telescopes so cumulative reward equals exactly the endpoint audit improvement — no policy can inflate reward indefinitely while true performance stagnates. · Finite-panel bound is horizon-free: cumulative empirical reward ≤ true improvement + 2Δ_n(F, δ), where Δ_n is the uniform audit deviation of the model class; adaptivity over time costs nothing once the sealed panel controls the class. · Lean 4 mechanization covers telescoping, the finite-audit bound, finite Gibbs, and entropy floor — unusually strong verification standard for an ML-theory paper. · Four identified failure modes that break the guarantee: (1) clipping progress, (2) scoring on the agent's own stream, (3) high-capacity model on a reusable panel, (4) neural classes that make Δ_n vacuous. · Experiments on ARC-TGI grid-transformation generators: finite-audit deviation scales empirically as n^{−0.527}; black-box scalar attacks on reusable audits stay below the 2Δ_n threshold with standard release defenses.

### [When Context Returns: Toward Robust Internalization in On-Policy Distillation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.11627) — 5/10

Notes: **Context-induced degradation** (CID): after on-policy distillation internalizes a system prompt or task hint, re-supplying that same context at inference time hurts accuracy versus no-context inference—even on instances the student already solves correctly. · **Proposed fix**: a consistency regularizer that anchors the student's no-context output with stop-gradient, then penalizes context-conditioned output via forward KL divergence—costs exactly one extra forward pass per training step. · **Empirical breadth**: evaluated across 12 configurations (diverse domains + model families); improves context-conditioned accuracy in the majority, reduces CID in 11/12, and eliminates response-length inflation. · **Mechanistic evidence**: hidden-state analysis confirms the fix works at the representation level—activations are nearly identical with or without context present after regularization. · **Caveat**: evaluation relies on the authors' own 12 configurations; no independent replication or ablation against alternative regularization strategies is described in the abstract.
