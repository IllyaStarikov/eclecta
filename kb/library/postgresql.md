- **2026-06-21** — [PostgresBench: A Reproducible Benchmark for Postgres Services](https://clickhouse.com/blog/postgresbench) — ClickHouse has published the first reproducible, open methodology for comparing managed Postgres OLTP throughput across providers, but readers should weigh that the benchmark's author is also its top-performing entrant and that a material storage hardware asymmetry favors the vendor's own service.
